About Me

Hi, I'm Raj

I graduated from UC Santa Cruz with a degree in Computer Science. My passion for teaching stems from my own 10th-grade physics teacher, who showed me that learning doesn't have to be intimidating.

Looking for dedicated support in math, physics, or other subjects? As an experienced local tutor to the Bay Area, I offer personalized online and in-person sessions tailored to your student's needs, covering everything from standard high school math to advanced AP courses.

I have experience tutoring 40+ students in all grades from 3rd-12th as well as university students. My goal is to build genuine confidence and deep understanding through clear, structured guidance personalized for your student’s needs.
